It's a scalable and secure data platform that allows enterprises to ingest, store, process, and analyze any type or volume of information.
A data lake<span style="color:rgb(216, 203, 251)"> is a capture of every aspect of a business operation</span>. The data is stored in its <span style="color:rgb(216, 203, 251)">natural/raw format</span>, usually as object blobs or files.
- Retain all data in its native format.
- Support all data types and all users.
- Adapt to changes easily.
- The format tends to be application-specific.
