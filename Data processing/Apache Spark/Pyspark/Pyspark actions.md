To <span style="color:rgb(216, 203, 251)">trigger the computation of our logical transformation</span> plan we run an action.
- An action instructs Spark to <span style="color:rgb(216, 203, 251)">compute a result from a series of transformations</span>.
```python
divisBy2.count()
```
Types of actions:
- Actions to <span style="color:rgb(216, 203, 251)">view data</span> in the console.
- Actions to <span style="color:rgb(216, 203, 251)">collect data to native objects</span> in the respective language.
- Actions to <span style="color:rgb(216, 203, 251)">write to output</span> data sources.
